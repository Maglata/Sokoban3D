Important Info:

E - Empty Space

X - Wall

P - Player

B - Box

T - Target

b - Box on a Target

p - Player on a Target

Width and Height are defined on the next line in that order

All lines must be separated by an enter

Rules:
1. There can be only 1 player
2. There must be at least 1 target and 1 box
3. The number of targets and boxes must be equal
